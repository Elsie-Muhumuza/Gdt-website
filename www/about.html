About us

Stewards of the Equator

At Goddidthis Tropicals, we believe that the fertile volcanic soils of the Ugandan Equator are a divine gift. Our mission is to be the Stewards of the Equator, managing this abundance with technical precision and structural integrity.

Founded by a Civil Engineer, we bridge the gap between smallholder heritage and global export standards. By partnering with local farmers, we ensure that every slice of fruit is traceable to the source, solar-dehydrated for purity, and handled with the honor it deserves.

We don’t just export snacks; we engineer a supply chain of integrity.
